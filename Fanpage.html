About the band

Dance Gavin Dance is an American rock band originating from Sacramento, California, formed in 2005. They have a total of 9 albums, with a tenth currently being recorded. With 3 albums in the American top-twenty, this accomplished band has exploded in popularity in the latter half of the 2010s.
